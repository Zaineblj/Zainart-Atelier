ZAINEB LAALJI

About

the Artist

❛ Art is my voice, it provides me a sense of freedom, freedom to live multiple peaceful lives, far from wars and politics.

Hey, my name is Zaineb,
I am many things, but most of all, I'm an artist.
I love the sound of heavy rain, the sparkling sunset waves,
the moonlight shadow, cloudy sky, Marrakesh colorful souks, and beautiful doors.
I am inspired by nature, Moroccan aesthetic, boho-chic and vintage style.

Initially, I practiced art as a way to find solace and heal myself, it began when I was sixteen, but over time it transformed into a way to help others heal as well.
It is a way for me to connect with people’s souls, to bring joy and positive energy to their homes, through my bohemian and handmade home decor items, created with love and passion.❜
